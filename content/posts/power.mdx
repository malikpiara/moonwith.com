People like you and me have more power to effect change and influence the world than we would have had at any other time in human history.

There are at least a handful of people around you and on the internet who are willing to listen to you. They trust your judgement. When you use your voice, you influence their action. You create ripples. You encourage them to follow suit. Are you using your power?
