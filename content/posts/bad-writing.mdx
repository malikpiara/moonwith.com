---
title: Show me your bad writing
description: Where is your bad work?
date: "2023-11-05"
---

Being bad at something is the excuse many of us like to use to avoid doing the creative work. If you're such a bad writer, painter, musician where is your bad work?

Seth Godin has been writing a blog post every single day for over 20 years. It's not a coincidence he's one of the most influential and insightful authors in business and marketing today.

We can do bad work and put it out there. Even if we're not proud of it. As it turns out, that is the only way to eventually do work that is good or even great.
